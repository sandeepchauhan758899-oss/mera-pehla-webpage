Jalvayu Parivartan: Ek Gambhir Chunauti

Jalvayu parivartan (Climate Change) aaj poori duniya ke liye ek aisa khatra ban gaya hai jis par dhyan dena bahut zaroori hai. Iska matlab sirf garmi ka badhna nahi hai, balki hamari dharti ke poore ecosystem ka bigadna hai. Jab hum fossil fuels jaise koyla, petrol aur gas jalate hain, toh unse nikli greenhouse gases dharti ke tapman ko badha deti hain.

Iske Mukhya Kaaran:

  • Pedon ki Katai: Ped carbon dioxide sote hain, lekin unki katai se hawa mein zeher fail raha hai.
  • Pradushan: Factory aur gaadiyon se nikalne wala dhuwa hamari parat (Ozone layer) ko nuksan pahuncha raha hai.
  • Plastic ka Istemal: Plastic dharti aur samudra dono ko ganda kar raha hai.

Hamare Jeevan par Asar:

Climate change ki wajah se glaciers pighal rahe hain, jisse samudra ka star badh raha hai. Aane wale waqt mein kai sheher pani mein doob sakte hain. Saath hi, be-mausam ki baarish aur bhayankar sookha kheti ko tabah kar raha hai, jisse khane-peene ki killat ho sakti hai.

Hum Kya Kar Sakte Hain?

Humein apni aane wali peedhiyon ke liye aaj hi kadam uthane honge. Zyada se zyada ped lagana, solar energy ka upyog karna, aur bijli ki bachat karna bahut zaroori hai. Yaad rakhiye, hamare paas rehne ke liye sirf ek hi dharti hai!
